# Hurricane-Wilma

# Oceanographic Response to Hurricane Wilma in the Northern Quintana Roo Coastal Region

# Paper
Journal Ciencias del Mar UAS; Vol. 2 No. 2 (2025): Revista Ciencias del Mar UAS - Edición Especial; 76-95, Revista Ciencias del Mar UAS; Vol. 2 Núm. 2 (2025): Revista Ciencias del Mar UAS - Edición Especial; 76-95, 3061-8959, 3061-8959

This repository contains MATLAB workflows and environmental data processing examples developed to analyze oceanographic changes associated with the passage of Hurricane Wilma in the northern coastal region of Quintana Roo, Mexico.

The study area includes:
- Cancún,
- Playa del Carmen,
- Cozumel.

The analyses focus on environmental variability observed after approximately 56 hours of hurricane influence, including:
- sea surface salinity (SSS),
- sea surface temperature (SST),
- chlorophyll-a (Chl).

The workflows include:
- preprocessing of NetCDF, CSV, and MATLAB datasets,
- regional environmental analysis,
- before-and-after comparisons,
- statistical assessment of variable changes,
- visualization and mapping routines.
